## Recommended Outreach Workflow

1. **Tier-A (Top 20):** Send personalized email to listed address + LinkedIn DM to a Research Head / Founder
2. **Tier-B (Mid-size brokerages):** Apply via the careers page AND email the listed `careers@`/`hr@` address
3. **Tier-C (Large firms):** Apply only via official portal — cold email is wasted effort
4. **Always attach:** 1-page CV + 1-page sample equity report on a stock you've researched
5. **Cadence:** Send batches of 10/day, follow up after 7 days, then 14 days
