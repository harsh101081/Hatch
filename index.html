About the Company

A diversified financial services group built on research & trust.

Founded in 1987, Motilal Oswal Financial Services Ltd. has grown into one of India's most recognised financial brands. The company operates across broking, wealth management, asset management, investment advisory, research, and a wide range of financial products — serving retail investors, traders, HNIs and institutions.

  • Broking & DistributionStocks, derivatives, commodities, mutual funds, IPOs and bonds.
  • Wealth & Asset ManagementPMS, AIFs, mutual funds and personalised wealth planning.
  • Research & AdvisoryAward-winning research, ideas and advisory across asset classes.
