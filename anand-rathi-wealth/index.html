01 The Company at a Glance

A house built
around your wealth

An advisory practice where every recommendation begins with understanding the person behind the portfolio.

Anand Rathi Wealth Ltd. is a private wealth management and investment advisory firm. We help clients make calm, well-informed decisions about their money — combining financial planning, investment advisory and dedicated relationship management into one trusted relationship.

Rather than selling products, we begin with your life: your goals, your responsibilities and the future you wish to secure. From there we shape strategies that protect capital, pursue sensible growth, and adapt as your circumstances evolve over the years.

Why the Company Exists

Purpose, before
products

We exist for a simple reason: to help clients protect, manage and grow their wealth with guidance they can trust. Three commitments shape everything we do.

i.

Preserve Wealth

Safeguarding what you have earned through prudent, risk-aware planning — so the foundation you have built remains secure for those who depend on it.

ii.

Plan Future Goals

Translating ambitions — education, retirement, legacy — into structured, achievable plans, with clear milestones and the flexibility to adapt over time.

iii.

Grow Capital Responsibly

Pursuing meaningful, long-term growth through suitable, well-researched solutions — never speculation, always considered and aligned to your comfort.
