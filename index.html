About the Company

A digital home for investing & trading

Angel One Ltd. is a digital-first financial services and stockbroking company. It brings the stock market and a full suite of financial products into one simple, technology-led experience.

Through its app and online platform, Angel One lets people buy and sell stocks, trade in futures & options, invest in mutual funds, apply for IPOs and explore commodities — supported by research, advisory tools and clear guidance at every step.
